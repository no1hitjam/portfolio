Canvasser   

Genre: Strategy
Released: 2013
Language: ActionScript 3.0
Designer, Artist, Programmer, Writer, Composer: Jackson Lango

Grab your clipboard and brush up on your social skills: It’s time to raise money on the streets for charity! Canvasser is a persuasion game, and it’s all about getting the most donations possible before the day ends. Can you sustain interest, trust, and get your town to care about Evergreen Forest before it’s too late?

It's A Monster!   

Genre: Brawler
Released: 2010
Language: ActionScript 2.0
Designer, Artist, Programmer, Composer: Jackson Lango

This Beat ‘Em Up game has a unique twist: You can pick, throw, and eat anything you see! This game features lively hand-drawn characters and varied locales like a dummy factory, a psychedelic mini-world and a refrigerator! The fighting is fast paced, the bosses have strategic weak points, and everything is kind of crazy.
